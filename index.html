Preparation

Data Sources


  • IMDB: IMDB is an online database of movie actors, movies, TV shows, TV entertainers, video games, and movie production teams.










  • TMDB: TMDB is a community-built online database of movies, TV shows, cast and crew.

Preparation Steps


Before formally producing the system, we have carried out the following preparatory work:


  1. Download and Organize Data: We downloaded the data in xlsx format from IMDB and consulted TMDB to supplement the incomplete data.

  2. Format Conversion: We used xlsx.full.min.js to convert the data format from xlsx to js.














  3. Start: We started to design the system with the processed data.
